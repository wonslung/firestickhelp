Frequently Asked Questions

How do i register my Fire Stick to my Amazon account?

  1. Make sure you have setup wifi
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Navigate to My Account and press the select button on your remote
  4. Press the select button on your remote to confirm
  5. Enter in your Amazon account information

Do I need an Amazon Account to use a wonslung Fire Stick?

I set up each wonslung Fire Stick with a placeholder amazon account. You do not need to set up an Amazon account to use kodi, however if you wish to use Amazon Prime Video, Netflix or any other offical App, you must use an amazon account. (see Registering my Fire Stick to my Amazon Account)

Why am I being asked to rent or buy a video?

Amazon sells this device as a viewing device for Amazon Video. Much of Amazon Video requires an Amazon Prime Membership. You are probably looking for Kodi. If you have an Amazon Prime account or a netflix account (see Registering my Fire Stick to my Amazon Account)

What is this message asking me if i want to update my build? (What is wonslung Wizard)

Generally speaking, when everything is working the way it’s supposed to, you shouldn’t NEED to update. However, sometimes addons change, other times they stop working. I periodically cut a new build to fix these issues and add new features (or remove broken addons). Due to this, i built in a build wizard, (wonslung Wizard). (documentation yet to be completed)
